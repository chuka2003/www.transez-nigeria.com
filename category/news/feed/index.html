The Investment forum is being organized by the Nigerian Investment Promotion Commission with the theme “Nigeria – China Economic Relations; building on the momentum”

The forum will focus on the following sectors that are part of the priority areas of the present Administration of President Goodluck Ebele Jonathan, GCFR

* Construction
* Banking and Finance
* Agriculture/ Agric Business
* Tourism
* Solid Minerals
* lCT
* Transport/Aviation

* Oil & Gas
