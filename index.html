Our Offerings

  • Quality Power Up-Time

    Power quality has become increasingly important for industrial and commercial electric power customers, particularly as today’s information systems and control processes rely on computerized equipment which is sensitive to power system interruptions and disturbances. Transez can provide solutions to existing power quality problems, alleviate problems that may be looming in a power system, and provide recommendations to ensure optimal power quality for facilities under construction or renovation.

  • Generator Maintenance Service

    Our team of qualified and experienced engineers are very capable of carrying out the most difficult and complex aspect of generator repairs. Our engineers have undergone various local and international training on various types of electrical generating engines. Their experiences vary from 5 to 20 years. We do not only repair and service, but specialize in complete overhauling of the entire generating engine system to give the generator a new lease of life.

  • Energy Fuel Supply

    We shall manage our diesel storage and distribution system optimally in accordance with the existing energy fueling policy of your organization. Our 15,000 litre trucks in our fleet shall be mobilized to serve your locations and we lease new ones as the needs arise. All trucks in our fleet are fitted with 4 nos of steel wired hose reel with a minimum length of 200m.
    Our supplies shall always be measured in accordance with the calibration of site tanks and hourly consumption of the power generators at each site. Our utmost objective shall to maintain availability of fuel at your locations at all times 24/7 365 days a year

  • Utility Power Management

    Transez has extensive experience in supporting and managing utility power from PHCN. We will manage all sites for the availability of PHCN power by timely bills settlement, working closely with local officials and ensuring that each cell sites transformers are in good working conditions all the time. Our power support services will also include the development and implementation of remote monitoring solution for prepaid meters, ensuring that any overbilling is captured and resolved.
